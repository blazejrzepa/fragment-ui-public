# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project harassment-free for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume good intent
- Give and accept feedback gracefully
- Focus on what's best for the community

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech
- Personal attacks or insults
- Publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove or edit contributions, comments, or issues that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

To report a concern, please contact: conduct@fragment-ui.dev
